## 🎨 皮肤融合词（精简版）

> ⚠️ **v4.0**：四层体系压缩为 **核心必加** + **场景可选**。

### 🔒 核心融合（每个 prompt 必加）

```
realistic tattoo, ink embedded in dermis, tattoo beneath skin surface,
follows body contours, slightly faded edges, pores visible through ink
```

这 6 个词解决 90% 的「贴纸感」问题。

### 🎯 场景可选（按需加 1-2 个）

| 场景 | 加词 |
|------|------|
| 旧纹身/陈年感 | `aged tattoo, ink slightly blurred, weathered appearance` |
| 紧身衣物遮挡 | `tattoo partially visible under tight clothing, fabric pressing on ink` |
| 汗湿皮肤 | `tattoo visible through sweat sheen, ink catches light differently` |
| 烙印效果 | `raised scar texture, skin-tone indent, subtle brand mark` |

### ⛔ 负面排除（精简 1 行）

```
no sticker, no decal, no flat tattoo, no floating on skin, no computer font, no illegible scribble, no garbled text
```
